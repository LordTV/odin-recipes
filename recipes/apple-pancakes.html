Apple Pancakes

Description

Delicious Apple Pancakes for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar or to taste
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon or to taste
  • 1 cup milk or as needed
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup shredded tart apple
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and cinnamon together in a large bowl.
  3. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth. Stir grated apple into pancake batter until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining pancake batter.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
