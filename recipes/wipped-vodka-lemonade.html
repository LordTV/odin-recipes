Whipped Vodka Lemonade

Description

Whipped Vodka Lemonade cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup limoncello cream liqueur
  • 1/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 fluid ounces lemon and elderflower vodka
  • 2 cups ice, or as needed
  • vanilla whipped cream (optional)
  • lemon zest
  • lemon wheels, dried or fresh (optional)
  1. Combine limoncello cream, lemon juice, sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, and lemon & elderflower vodka in a blender. Add ice; blend on Whip or Frozen Cocktail setting until ice is blended.
  2. Pour into glasses; garnish with vanilla whipped cream, lemon zest, and dried or fresh lemon wheel.
